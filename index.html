Welcome to "Sumbonganan sa Katawhan"

your trusted source for the latest happenings and news in the picturesque region of Misamis Oriental, Philippines!

  • Our Channel is Your Window to the Heart of Misamis Oriental.
  • Our channel is dedicated to keeping you well-informed about the events and stories that hold significance for the people of this beautiful region.
  • From breaking news to local community gatherings, we strive to capture the essence of Misamis Oriental's pulse.

Beyond news, we showcase interviews with local personalities and community leaders. Our channel aims to shed light on the vibrant and diverse communities within Misamis Oriental, giving you an insight into the richness of our region's culture.

Dedicated Reporting

The team is composed of experienced reporters and journalists who are deeply rooted in the local community. We take pride in delivering reliable and accurate reporting on a diverse range of topics, encompassing politics, entertainment, sports, culture, and more.

Your Voice Matters

"Sumbonganan sa Katawhan" firmly believes that every individual has a unique voice and perspective. We are dedicated to providing a platform for all members of the community to share their stories and viewpoints.

Meet Our Vibrant Community

Our channel is also a platform to highlight the vibrant and diverse communities that call this region home. We take pride in celebrating the unique traditions, cultures, and events that make Misamis Oriental so special.

Trust in Every Story

At "Sumbonganan sa Katawhan," trust is the cornerstone of our commitment to you. We take pride in being your trusted source for news and stories that matter in Misamis Oriental. Our dedication to accuracy, reliability, and transparency ensures that you can rely on us for the most trustworthy information about our region.
